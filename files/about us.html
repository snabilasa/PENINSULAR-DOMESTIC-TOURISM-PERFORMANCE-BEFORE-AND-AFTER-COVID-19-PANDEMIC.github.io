ABOUT US

Cuti-Cuti Malaysia (CCM) is one of the most respected and successful names in the Malaysia travel industry and comprehensive guide to the Malaysia best travel destinations. CCM is dedicated to the provision of objective, accurate, informative and reliable travel content in various formats, including the World Travel Guide for consumers, Malaysia Travel Professional for travel industry professionals and extensive licensed and bespoke content for commercial partners through its specialist division. Besides, it provides detailed and accurate travel content designed to inspire local and global travelers. It covers all aspects such as accommodation, transportation, worship, food and beverage, and current COVID-19 cases in Peninsular Malaysia. Updated every year by a dedicated global editorial team, the portal logs one million users monthly.
